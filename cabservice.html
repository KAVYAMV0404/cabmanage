Cab Management & Reservation Framework is a cloud and mobile ready solution that offers the basic reservation building blocks to create a customized cab booking solution. The framework contains customized plug-n-play modules like booking engine, fleet management, payment management and user management. It also offers elements such as an administration interface where the cab company can manage the content, and access all bookings and customer information.

Our Cab Service Management System Framework helps taxi service providers deliver streamlined taxi booking experience through an intuitive customer mobile app and a customized driver app. The framework simplifies fleet management and helps the enterprise focus on improving their customer service without worrying about the technical integrity.
